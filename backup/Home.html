Welcome to  Domaine Saint-Bernard

The Domaine Saint-Bernard is a green space recognized as ‘’community park devoted to ecotourism’’. It is preserved to perpetuity by the Domaine Saint-Bernard Board of Trustees. An ecosystem covering 1,500 acres, its natural beauty and peacefulness will charm you.  It as much a great place to vist and spend time in summer as well as in winter. Many social and cultural activities take place throughout the year.
